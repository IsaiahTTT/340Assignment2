Darth Bane

Founder of the Rule of Two, responsible for the demise of Lord Kaan's brotherhood of darkness and the birth of the new Sith Order he invisions through the "Rule of Two".

Darth Zannah

The first heir to the Rule of Two, trained by Darth Bane from a young age in the ways and teachings of the sith.

Darth Cognus

Skilled Assassin known as the Huntress with a genetic gift in foresight. Originally trained under Darth Bane. When Bane was slain by Darth Zannah she pledged alligaince to Zannahs teaching and became her apprentice.

Darth Plagueis

The second to last rule of two master known as Darth Plagueius the Wise, Apprentice to Darth Teneborous and Master of Darth Sidious. began pulling the strings for the sith grand plan using his public identity Hego Damask to gain wealth and a political standing.

Darth Sidious

The last rule of two master, executed the sith grand plan to take over the galaxy and destroy the jedi with order 66 using his public identity as Senator Palpatine to gain political power and influence. He was the apprentice of Darth Plagueis and trained Darth Maul, Count Dooku, and gave rise to Darth Vader through his manipulation of Jedi Knight Anakin Skywalker.
